Become an iCitizen

Get involved with your government


Polls

Go to the polls! Seriously though, voting responsibly allows you to make a direct impact on your local and global surroudings. By voting for your preferred legislators and answering questions on referendums, you can actively voice your opinions and qualms about the state of government. We fetch poll information using the Google Civic Information API to help you become aware of polls taking place near you. You can save polls as well, allowing you to revisit just the ones that interest you.

Events

Attending political events is a great way to stay in touch with the community and remain aware of pressing matters. We use machine learning to aggregate events from Eventbrite that will be most relevant to you, taking out the hassle of finding those events. You can even save events, so you can be sure never to miss out on the ones that interest you the most.

Legislators

Above all people, your national and state legislators make the biggest impact on government. It can be difficult to find and stay on top of what your legislators are up to. We make this task easier by finding and putting all of your legislators in one place, providing you with avenues for contacting them as well.
All legislator data is obtained from the Propublica and OpenStates APIs.

Bills

You might be curious as to what bills your legislators introduce to make changes happen. This information is rather obscure for the layperson and difficult to obtain, so we search for bills using the subjects you are interested in. We make additional bill information easy for you to obtain, and you can also save bills of interest to you. Don't stay in the dark!
All bill information is obtained from the Propublica and OpenStates APIs.
